Europe Can Be Liked · ECBL

Erasmus+ Project

An Erasmus+ project on European citizenship involving Italy, Germany, Latvia and Poland.

About the ECBL Project

"Europe Can Be Liked" (ECBL) is an Erasmus+ cooperation project for adult education. It contrasts Euroscepticism — especially among the younger generation — by drawing on the memories and experience of senior citizens.

By combining historical knowledge with modern teaching methods, the project promotes awareness of the European Union and strengthens a shared European identity through workshops, publications, public debates and study visits.

Duration 2025 – 2027 24 months
Action Erasmus+ KA220-ADU Adult Education Partnership
Priority Common values Civic engagement & participation

Project Objectives

  • Strengthen a sense of European identity among senior citizens and other participants.
  • Encourage seniors' social activation and participation in public life.
  • Develop innovative educational materials about the European Union.
  • Collect and publish seniors' memories of their countries' entry into the EU.
  • Promote dialogue between generations through public debates and workshops.

The Four Partners

The project unites four organisations active in adult education and senior engagement across Europe.

Lead partner

Fundacja "Aktywni XXI"

Jelenia Góra, Poland

The Future of the European Union

  • The challenges the EU must face: migration, Brexit, climate change.
  • EU enlargement and the future of European integration.
  • The role of young people in shaping the future of Europe.
  • The EU facing globalisation and new technologies.

LUTE Milazzo

Milazzo (ME), Italy

The Institutions and Functioning of the European Union

  • The EU bodies: Parliament, European Council, Commission, Court of Justice and others.
  • The EU decision-making process, from idea to law.
  • The EU budget and its impact on citizens' lives.
  • Citizens' participation in the political life of the EU.

Seniorenbüro Hamburg e.V.

Hamburg, Germany

The European Union: from Idea to Reality

  • The history of European integration: from the beginnings to the present day.
  • The EU treaties: the founding documents that shape the Union.
  • The values and objectives of the European Union.
  • The role of individual member states in the creation of the EU.

Preiļu novada Izglītības pārvalde

Preiļi, Latvia

The European Union and Everyday Life

  • The free movement of persons, goods, services and capital.
  • Regional policy and EU funds.
  • Environmental protection and sustainable development.
  • International cooperation and the EU's role in the world.

What We Do

Workshop Guide

A methodological guide with workshop scenarios on the EU, designed for trainers and educators working with seniors.

Workshops "European ABC"

Educational workshops for senior citizens, covering the history, institutions, values and daily impact of the European Union.

Voices from the Past

A collection of senior citizens' memoirs about their countries' entry into the EU, to share knowledge between generations.

Study Visits

Visits to EU institutions in Brussels and Strasbourg, to experience first-hand where European decisions are made.

Expected Results

  • Greater awareness of the European Union and its values.
  • A stronger sense of shared European identity among senior citizens.
  • Increased civic participation and trust in European institutions.
  • Reduction of Euroscepticism through evidence-based education.
  • High-quality educational materials reusable by other organisations.
